About Us

Vision

To be leading Centre of Excellence in Aviation Medicine in Botswana, Africa and among the best in the world by 2030.

Mission

A pioneering company in Botswana that provides professional, highly specialized, and well-rounded advanced aviation medical services to aviation personnel (air & ground crew), ensuring adherence to aviation medical standards by employing internationally recognised approaches and practices in caring for the human aspect of the aviation system, thereby, playing a major role in aviation safety.

Values

Our values are Botho, professionalism, quality of service, reliability, integrity, and transparency.

Aviation Medical Services

OUT-PATIENT CARE

  • Screening and continuous care for diabetes, high blood pressure & other chronic illnesses.

SPECIALIST AVIATION MEDICINE SERVICES

  • Aviation medical research activities and drawing of aircrew health & safety policies & procedural manuals.
  • Aircrew medical surveillance.
  • Pilot wellness and medical program (PWMP) consultancy.
  • All types of air and ground crew medical examinations and assessments.
  • Consultancy in Human Systems Integration (HSI).
  • Aircrew Training: inclusive of refreshers: aviation medicine training programs for aircrew (Indoctrination, aircrew health & safety education programs, etc.).
  • Health-care personnel training: safe aeromedical evacuation (Flight Nurses and flight nurse assistants).
  • Medical intelligence prior to air travel.
  • Consultancy in safe aeromedical evacuations: (expert advice).
  • Active aeromedical evacuation services: active participation as part of the airlifting team on request.
  • Consultancy in aircraft incidents/accidents investigations as a human factors consultant.
  • Aviation Psychology Services (Mental health and wellness for aviation personnel).

OUR TEAM

Dr. Nako-Phuthego

Dr. O Nako-Phuthego holds a Doctor of Medicine (MD), with Postgraduate training in Aerospace Medicine from RAF-CAM and King's College London (Masters Degree Aerospace Medicine).
Founder & Director of Aeromed Prime Solutions Pty Ltd, a pioneering company based in Botswana, Gaborone, offering consultancy services in advanvced Aerospace medicine (both civil and military). Aeromed Prime Solutions also houses Innovative Clinic which offers primary healthcare services. Aeromed Prime Consultancy is also registered with BQA.
With a distinguished military career, she served as a Military Doctor at a rank of Captain for seven years, with five of those years dedicated to being the Specialist Flight Surgeon within the Botswana Defence Force's Air Arm. During this time, she delivered "unique" to Aviation healthcare services to military aviation personnel.
Currently, Dr. Nako-Phuthego holds the esteemed position of Consultant Aero-Medical Assessor for the Civil Aviation Authority of Botswana. In this capacity, she oversees all aero-medical matters and concerns within Botswana's aviation industry, in alignment with the standards and recommended practices set by the International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO).
Dr. Nako-Phuthego's notable contributions include a feature in the Black History Month of the Royal Aeronautical Society in 2020. She currently co-authored a paper in Aerospace Physiology in the International Journal of Aerospace Medicine and Human Performance, published by the Aerospace Medical Association in February 2023.

Karabo Isaacs

Karabo Isaacs is a highly proficient and seasoned personal assistant, holding a degree in Business Administration with a specialization in Marketing from the esteemed University of Botswana. In her role as a personal assistant to Dr. Nako-Phuthego, Karabo consistently exhibits a remarkable level of professionalism, impeccable organizational skills, and an unwavering commitment to precision and detail.
